About this PoC

Automatic, connected-speech references

Voices, pronunciations, tone annotations, alignments, and selections were generated or inferred automatically and have not been reviewed by a Mandarin speaker.

Solid contours are extracted from a selected recording. Small tone glyphs are only citation-form reminders. The six-model envelope is not a native-speaker population norm.

No TTS, ASR, alignment, or learner audio is sent to a service by the deployed application.
